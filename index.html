NeuroHue Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your NeuroHue account and associated data, please email us at contact@neurohue.world with the following information:

  • Subject line: "NeuroHue Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • User role (parent, therapist, etc.)

What happens when you delete your account?

Upon processing your request, we will:

  • Delete your personal information including name, email, and contact details
  • Remove all associated child account data and progress information
  • Delete any activity history and usage data

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once completed.

Note: Some information may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
